Garrett Brown - Web Developer

About the dev

Garrett Brown is an aspiring coder currently enrolled with Epicodus. Garrett graduated in the class of 2016 from Reynolds High School, and initially wanted to pursue a theatre arts degree, as he had realized he had a passion for it just before he graduated. He went to school at Clackamas Community College, Mt. Hood Community College, and South Puget Sound Community College for 4 years. After realizing he didn't have a particular aim for what he was actually going to do after school, he took interest in coding as his cousin had been a graduate of Epicodus and quickly made progress in the field. Garrett took this inspiration and enrolled himself in hopes of finally finding a calling.

Garrett's current interests include video games, Dungeons and Dragons with his friends, drawing, playing guitar and bass, (Although he's been doing a bad job of actually picking them up lately.) with intentions of creating music. He also enjoys theatre arts and has acted in 7 shows on stage, as an extra in Documentary Now!, and even the webseries "Fallen Justice." He also acted in "Dinner Detective Portland", an improv-based murder-mystery company for 2 years. Some of his favorite bands include Sybreed, Periphery, Perturbator, Celldweller, and A Day to Remember, with metal being his favorite genre. He currently resides in Portland living with his awesome roommates, their cat, Bowie, and their dog, Forest.
